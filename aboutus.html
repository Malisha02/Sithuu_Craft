About Us

Sithuu Crafts is all about bringing warmth, creativity, and love to your home. We design handmade crochet clothes, flowers, teddies, and mandalas that make every moment special.

Our Mission: Spreading Handmade Happiness

Our mission is to spread joy through handcrafted creations. We believe in sustainable, thoughtful, and love-filled products that brighten your life and bring smiles to those you care about.

Our Story

What began as a small hobby has blossomed into a heartfelt journey of creativity. Every piece is handmade with care, inspired by the beauty of nature and the joy of giving. Our founder, Maleesha, turned her passion into a brand that celebrates craftsmanship and the art of crochet.
